Conditions

No restrictions with respect to age, nationality
or experience:
both beginners and professionals can take active
or passive part.

The participation will be confirmed after the application form, CV and the payment are received.

The choice of repertoire is free. Selected pieces are subject to confirmation. Here you may find the list of pieces chosen by other participants.

The language of the master class is English. All non-English speaking participants will be asked to inform us accordingly.

The instrumentation of the orchestra is: wood 2-2-2-2, brass 4 horns, 2 trumpets, 3 trombones, 1 tuba, percussion 2, harp 1, strings.

Michail Jurowski
